MEGA

Make Earth Great & Awesome(MEGA) is an online platform that supports innovation and invention. It was founded upon realization that many people have great ideas but fail to implement them.

The reasons for the above include lack of implementation strategy, lack of funds and lack of an avenue to showcase the idea.

MEGA lets users pitch their idea to the global audience with feedback on the viability of the idea and a chance for collaboration with other like minded megaminders from all over the world.

MEGA Collaboration

Our team uses MEGA analytics tools to establish the pitches with most traffic (impressions & interactions).During the annual MEGA EXPO, top ideas will be selected and the idea owners will be given a chance to pitch before renown venture capitalists and CEOs from top companies

We also encourage Megaminders (MEGA Users) to collaborate with each other. Network, connect and share ideas.

MEGApp mobile application is also available on playstore and apple store.Download to enjoy cool collaborative features
